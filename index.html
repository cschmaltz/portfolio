Project 01
AI Voice Agent: Inbound Call Handling, Booking and Deposit Collection
Live
Watch demo
Result Zero missed calls. Every inbound inquiry qualified, booked, and deposited without a human picking up the phone.

Built a fully automated AI voice agent that answers every inbound call for a service business, qualifies the caller, collects booking details, and sends a secure Stripe payment link via SMS. No human involvement required at any stage of the process.

The agent handles the entire conversation naturally using ElevenLabs conversational AI. Once booking details are collected, n8n triggers automatically: it generates a Stripe payment link, sends it via Twilio SMS, and waits for payment confirmation. When the customer pays, Stripe webhooks back to n8n, which books the appointment in Google Calendar, updates the tracking sheet, and sends a confirmation SMS.

Works for any service business that takes appointments and deposits: salons, barbershops, HVAC, plumbers, law firms, and medical clinics.

ElevenLabs n8n Stripe Twilio Google Calendar

Project 04
Daily Content Intelligence Pipeline: 6 Platforms, 8am, No Human Input
Live
Watch demo
Result 4 hours of daily manual content work reduced to 4 minutes of review and approval. Running in production daily for months with zero manual intervention.

Fully automated intelligence and content pipeline running on a self-hosted n8n instance. Fires at 8am daily across 4 parallel scraping lanes: YouTube captions, Reddit threads, Google News via Serper, and AI-powered news search via Perplexity.

All intelligence is merged, analysed by OpenRouter, and passed to a content generation layer that produces a LinkedIn post, Facebook post, Reddit thread, Twitter line, Instagram carousel, TikTok script, Google My Business update, YouTube script, and nurture email. All outputs are sent to a human approval email with Approve and Reject buttons. Nothing posts without a human decision.

After approval, the system generates platform-specific images via Kie.ai, talking head videos via HeyGen, posts to all platforms via their respective APIs, and sends a confirmation email with all live links.

Project 06
RAG Document Query System: Ask Your Business Documents in Plain English
Live
View on Upwork
Result Businesses query their own product catalogs, compliance documents, and internal knowledge bases in plain English. Answers are sourced from actual business data, not a general model.

Built a Retrieval Augmented Generation pipeline that allows businesses to query their own documents in plain English. Ingests PDFs including product catalogs, compliance documents, and knowledge bases. Chunks them, generates embeddings via Gemini, and stores them in Pinecone.

Users ask questions in natural language. The system searches Pinecone for the most relevant context, passes it to Gemini, and returns accurate answers sourced from the business's own documents. No hallucinations. Answers are grounded in real data. No custom model training required.

Built for professional services clients who needed an AI that knows their specific products, policies, and processes rather than a general language model.

Extended voice agent architecture designed for businesses that handle both emergency and standard inbound calls: HVAC, plumbing, medical, and property management.

The agent opens every call with intent detection.
